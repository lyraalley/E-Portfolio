About this site

Welcome to my E-Portfolio! This website was created as a capstone project for my MLIS at Dominican University. Its purpose is to demonstrate the skills I have learned during my time in the program. Under the education section you will find a variety of artifacts of my work, sorted by the learning goals of the degree program as well as a reflective essay regarding my time in the program.

About Me

My passion lies with working with children and teens and providing a safe space were they can learn, grow, explore their interests, and develop their identities free of judgment. In the past I have particularly enjoyed doing programs on music and coding, as they are areas I am personally interested in. In my free time I enjoy reading, playing with my cats, making music, telling stories, playing video games, and customizing my computer.
